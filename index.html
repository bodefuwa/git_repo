How to Maximize HipChat's Value

  • Integration JIRA projects with HipChat
  • Integration Confluence spaces with HipChat
  • Drive discussions through HipChat instead of just emails
  • Create objective-specific rooms. Eg. Builds, Sprints, etc Note: No limit on creating rooms, but be practical
  • Active HipChat rooms can become noisy, use silencer Note: Turn off notifications to busy rooms such as jira activity and build notifications rooms
